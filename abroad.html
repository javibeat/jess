THRIVING ABROAD PROGRAM

That's why I designed "Thriving Abroad", a 12-week journey based on 6 pillars that are the key to get that more and better that we all dream of. Let me walk you through the program.

SELF-AWARENESS ▼

Before starting any journey you need to know your starting point and your destination. You will gain clarity about who you are, which are your values and you want to achieve in your life and career abroad.

BEHAVIORS AND BELIEFS ▼

Have you ever stopped to think how what you think affect your results? You will learn what is holding you back from living a fulfilling life and how to replace it with empowering mental and emotional routines.

EMOTIONS ▼

A healthy emotional management comes from knowing what and why you feel what you feel. Learning to listen to and decipher your emotions is a game-changer.

SELF-ESTEEM ▼

Self-esteem and self-confidence are two key elements to succeed and build your best life. How healthy is your self-image and how is it supporting or preventing your growth?

RELATIONSHIPS ▼

You might want to decide who you want to be part of your thriving journey and how this revolution is going to impact the people around you.

ROADMAP ▼

You are now ready to design your roadmap to keep building your dream life and career and creating your own thriving future.
