MEET YOUR COACH


Hello, I am Jessica!


I am a certified Life & Career Coach, specialized in Expats.

In a nutshell, I help those living outside their home country shape their experience abroad and make the most of it, both personally and professionally.


MY JOURNEY

You might be wondering how I ended up here.


Coaching got to my life in a moment where I was feeling completely lost for the first time ever.

I apparently had it all.

A shiny and bursty career as a Hotel Manager.

Living on one of the most beautiful islands in the Mediterranean.

But I felt that something was off.


My best friend suggested me a coach.

I honestly had no idea what to expect from that session but from that moment everything changed.

I got to know myself much better [and love me even more].

I landed the job of my dreams.

I met THE one.

I remember thinking "Wow, this coaching thing is really powerful! I want to help other people to experience the same".


And here I am, several trainings and certifications later, to support you in this journey.


Wondering if we might be the perfect match to work on your life and/or career?
